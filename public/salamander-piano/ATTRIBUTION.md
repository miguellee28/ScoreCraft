# Salamander Grand Piano samples

These MP3 samples are from the Salamander Grand Piano recording by Alexander
Holm, distributed by the Tone.js audio examples.

- Source: https://tonejs.github.io/audio/salamander/
- Upstream project: https://github.com/sfzinstruments/SalamanderGrandPiano
- License: Creative Commons Attribution 3.0

The files are stored locally so ScoreCraft playback does not need a network
connection after the application is installed.
